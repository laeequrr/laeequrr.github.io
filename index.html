The Story Behind the CV

My IT journey began in 2016 with a six-month internship at the Directorate of IT for the Government of Pakistan, where I built Android and desktop applications from scratch. That early exposure to software development gave me a deep appreciation for how technology is built — which continues to inform how I manage, secure and scale it today.

From the fast-paced hospitality environment of Dubai International Hotel to managing government-critical platforms at MOHRE, and from supporting six educational institutions at AlNajah to leading multi-campus IT operations at JESS — each chapter has added a new dimension to my expertise. I've delivered tenant migrations with zero downtime, built entire IT centres single-handedly and led MDM platform migrations without disrupting a single classroom.

What drives me is simple: technology should work seamlessly for the people who depend on it.
